About Me

I'm currently working at Lalilo, Paris as a Machine Learning Engineer, where I'm building data loading and transformation pipelines to maintain the product data platform. I'm also working on cutting-edge Speech AI models for children's voices, Natural Language Understanding, and Social Recommendation Systems while learning about the EdTech industry.


I started my career as a Data Engineer in the AI Factory of the National Bank of Canada in Montreal, where I developed automated data pipelines and deployed AI models in production. My role was to democratize AI and operationalize AI models with high quality standards: automation, monitoring, security, and scalability.


After 4 wonderful years at the National Bank of Canada, I decided to broaden my knowledge by joining MoovAI, Montreal as an ML Engineer. Moving beyond credit cards, fraud, and risk models, I explored other AI subjects to expand my expertise.


I then decided to return to France and joined Neovision, Grenoble, where I discovered exciting AI Computer Vision applications before ultimately joining my current role at Lalilo.

I've been working as a Data and ML Engineer in an Artificial Intelligence team for 7+ years now and I'm continuing my AI and data journey to learn more and apply this knowledge on various use cases for ethical, responsible and purposeful projects.
